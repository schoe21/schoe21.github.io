Raccoons are often seen as pests and associated with rabies, which is unfortunate. While it is true that a number of wild raccoons have rabies, the stigma that all raccoons have rabies or are dangerous is false.

Raccoons are even the mascot of the LA Zoo's recycling bins and the mascot of a minor league baseball team!
